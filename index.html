About Us

Welcome to Gr8 Hill Pool Villas

Tucked away in the peaceful countryside of Karanjale, Maharashtra, GR8 Hill Pool Villas was created as a home away from home where guests can relax, unwind, and indulge. Surrounded by rolling hills and fresh rural air, this retreat is a perfect blend of comfort and escape from the everyday. Here, mornings greet you with golden sunshine, afternoons invite you to lounge by the pool, and nights sparkle under a blanket of stars. With thoughtfully designed interiors, a private pool, and opportunities for scenic walks, local sightseeing, cycling, and riverside picnics, we’re certain that GR8 Hill Pool Villas has something special for every guest.
