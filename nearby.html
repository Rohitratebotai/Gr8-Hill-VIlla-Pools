Raja’s Seat, Coorg

A charming garden with stunning valley views, Raja’s Seat was once the favorite spot of Coorg’s kings. Located in Madikeri, this scenic viewpoint offers lush greenery, colorful flowers, and breathtaking sunsets....

Abbey Falls, Coorg

A scenic waterfall near Madikeri, Abbey Falls is surrounded by lush coffee plantations. The water cascades from 70 feet, creating a stunning view, especially during monsoons....

Madikeri Fort, Coorg

Built in the 17th century, Madikeri Fort is a historic landmark in the heart of Madikeri. Featuring stone ramparts, a palace-turned-museum, and a chapel...

Nagarhole National Park, Karnataka

A part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve, Nagarhole is a rich wildlife sanctuary known for its dense forests, elephants, tigers, and diverse birdlife...

General Thimayya Museum, Madikeri

Dedicated to General K.S. Thimayya, this museum showcases his life, military achievements, and artifacts like uniforms, medals, and a battle tank...

Golden Temple (Namdroling Monastery), Bylakuppe

A serene Tibetan monastery near Coorg, known for its golden Buddha statues and vibrant murals. It’s a peaceful spiritual retreat and a glimpse into Tibetan culture...

Chelavara Falls, Coorg

A stunning 150-foot waterfall near Virajpet, known for its scenic beauty and tortoise-shaped rock. Best visited during the monsoon, though swimming is not advised due to strong currents...

Kaveri Nisargadhama, Coorg

A serene island near Kushalnagar, this nature park is set along the Kaveri River. It features bamboo groves, a hanging bridge, boating, and an elephant ride...

Tala Kaveri, Coorg

The origin of the Kaveri River, Tala Kaveri is located on Brahmagiri Hill. The sacred tank marks the river's source, and the Talacauvery Temple is dedicated to Goddess Kaveramma...
