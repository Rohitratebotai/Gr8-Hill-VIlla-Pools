Shipping Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

Overview

At Gr8 Hill Pool Villas, we are committed to ensuring a smooth and reliable delivery experience for all merchandise, gift items, or vouchers ordered through our website. This Shipping Policy outlines how we process, ship, and manage product deliveries.

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 1–3 business days of confirmation. Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed the next working day. You will receive an order confirmation email once processing begins.

Shipping & Delivery

We currently deliver within India. Depending on your location and the selected shipping method, delivery may take 3–10 business days. Once shipped, you will receive tracking information via email or SMS.

Shipping Costs

Shipping charges are calculated at checkout and vary based on the delivery address and the type of product ordered. Occasionally, we may offer free shipping on select items or during promotional periods.

Delays

Although we strive for timely delivery, external factors such as weather, holidays, courier delays, or regional restrictions may cause occasional delays. We appreciate your understanding and will do our best to minimize any inconvenience.

Damaged or Lost Items

If your order arrives damaged or does not arrive within the estimated delivery period, please notify us within 7 days. We will coordinate with the courier service to resolve the issue and provide a replacement or refund, if applicable.
